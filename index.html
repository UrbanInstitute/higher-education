Funding of Public Higher Education
College affordability and access are national issues, but students in different states face different circumstances. Enrollment, funding, and tuition in public colleges and universities vary widely across states, and have evolved with states’ economies and policy priorities.

About this project

The nonprofit Urban Institute is dedicated to elevating the debate on social and economic policy. Urban receives funding from the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation as part of ongoing projects of the Urban Institute State and Local Finance Initiative and from Lumina Foundation to support ongoing work on higher-education affordability. Funders do not determine research findings or influence scholars’ conclusions.
